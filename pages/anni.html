ROYAL OAK
PERPETUAL
CALENDAR "150TH ANNIVERSARY"

LIMITED EDITION OF 150 Ref. 26585XT.OO.1220XT.01

Price on Request

Plan an Appointment

Connecting past and present, this vintage-inspired
timepiece pays a final tribute to Calibre 5135, the historic
openworked perpetual calendar movement, while
combining titanium and BMG (Bulk Metallic Glass) on the
case and bracelet for a limited edition of 150 pieces
featuring the special anniversary design codes.
CASE


The 41 mm case combines a bezel and caseback frame in BMG (Bulk Metallic Glass) with a titanium case middle and screw-locked crown. The timepiece is water resistant to 20 m.

SIZE
41 mm

THICKNESS
9.9 mm

WATER RESISTANCE
20 m

DIAL


The sapphire dial is fitted with blue counter surrounds, a matching inner bezel, 18-carat white gold round hour-markers and blue bathtub hands filled with luminescent material.
BRACELET


The integrated bracelet combines BMG studs with titanium links and an AP folding clasp.
